Our Love Story

How We Met

Spring 2018

Our story began on a beautiful spring day at the local coffee shop. Sarah was reading her favorite book when James accidentally bumped into her table, spilling coffee everywhere. What started as an embarrassing moment turned into hours of conversation and the beginning of something magical.

First Date

May 2018

Our first official date was a picnic in Central Park. James brought homemade sandwiches (which were surprisingly good!), and Sarah brought her guitar. We spent the entire afternoon talking, laughing, and Sarah even played a few songs. It was perfect.

The Proposal

December 2023

James proposed during our winter vacation in the mountains. After a long hike to our favorite viewpoint, he got down on one knee just as the sun was setting. With tears of joy and a resounding "YES!", we knew our forever had officially begun.
